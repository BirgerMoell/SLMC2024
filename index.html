About the Conference

Join leading researchers and practitioners to discuss advancements, challenges, and future directions in medical language understanding. The conference takes place on September 30th, 2024, bringing together experts in Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models in healthcare.

Conference Topics

NLP in Clinical Decision Support

Explore how NLP is revolutionizing clinical decision-making processes.

Medical Language Models

Discover the latest advancements in language models tailored for healthcare applications.

Ethical AI in Healthcare

Discuss the ethical implications of AI and NLP in medical contexts.
